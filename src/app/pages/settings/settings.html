local_cafe About Tea Mitra

Tea Mitra replaces the paper notebook the office uses to track daily tea and coffee deliveries. Every order is recorded with its price at the time, payments to the vendor are logged separately, and the pending balance is calculated automatically — no month-end manual counting required.
